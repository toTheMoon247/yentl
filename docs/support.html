Common questions

How does Yentl work?

Build your profile, and our matchmakers hand-pick introductions for you. When you and a match both accept, each of you unlocks the conversation with a one-time fee, and the chat opens.

How do I delete my account or download my data?

In the app, go to Profile → the gear icon → Account & Privacy. There you can Download my data or Delete account (deletion permanently erases your profile, photos, matches, and messages).

How do payments work?

Yentl is free to download. You only pay a small one-time fee to unlock the conversation with a match you both accepted — there are no subscriptions.

How do I report someone or get help with safety?

You can report or block another user from within a chat. For anything else, email us at the address above.
